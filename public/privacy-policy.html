CINETECA — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

CINETECA ("the app", "we") is a personal movie & TV tracker. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and the control you have over it.

Data we collect

  • Account data — when you sign in with Google we receive your name, profile photo and email address, used only to create and secure your account.
  • Content you create — the titles you mark as watched, your watchlist, ratings, lists, episode progress, reviews and the people/accounts you follow.

How we use it

Solely to provide the app's features: saving and syncing your library across devices, showing your public profile and reviews to other signed-in users, and generating your taste insights. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Where it is stored

Your data is stored with Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore), which acts as our infrastructure provider, with encryption in transit. Movie and TV information is provided by The Movie Database (TMDB); this product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

What is visible to others

Your display name, photo, reviews and review counts are visible to other signed-in users. Your watched library, watchlist, ratings and lists are private. Your email address is never shown to other users.

Your rights & deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Delete account. This removes your library, watchlist, lists, episode progress, reviews and profile (GDPR Article 17). For any data request, contact us below.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the date above.
